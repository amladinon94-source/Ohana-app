# Ohana — Product Context

register: product

## Product Purpose
Ohana is a local, no-cloud HTML/frontend previewer with superpowers for designers who direct an AI agent from an embedded terminal. "Framer for complex products": the designer decides, the agent builds in real code, Ohana is the visual channel between them (live preview, Figma-style comments, Moka flow boards, design.md as source of truth, handoff to real repos).

## Users
Product/UX designers working on dense B2B apps (reference: Simetrik-scale operational tools). Fluent in Figma, Linear, Notion, Arc. They live in this app for hours while iterating with an agent; speed and calm matter more than spectacle.

## Brand & Tone
Quiet, precise, opinionated. The tool disappears into the task. Dark, dense-but-calm chrome around the user's own content (the preview is the hero). Quality bar: Linear / Notion / Arc / Figma.

## Anti-references
- SaaS marketing gloss (gradients, glassmorphism, hero metrics).
- Decorative motion, page-load choreography, bouncy easing.
- Anything that competes visually with the previewed prototype.

## Strategic principles
- The designer decides; the AI expands; the designer filters.
- Everything the agent does must be visible live (file watching, pins, boards).
- One folder = one project; artifacts are plain files (markdown/JSON) the agent reads and writes.
